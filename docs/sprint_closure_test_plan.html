1. Objective & QA Scope

The objective of this testing matrix is to achieve 100% QA sign-off on the core messaging module. This ensures real-time reliability, media processing compatibility, correct channel rendering contexts, and mobile resource efficiency. Testing runs concurrently across three distinct layers:

2. Feature Assignment Matrix

Tester Module Areas Verification Focus
Rayne Sockets, Presence, & Real-Time Sync Auto-reconnection logic, typing events, active status dots, inline message deletion.
Islam Voice Messages, Media uploads & Cache Audio permission handler, Cloudinary file uploads, offline SQLite/AsyncStorage loading.
Yassine Header Logic, Channels & Layout UI Uppercase 'DM' channel context, partner profile details fetched, 75% bubble alignment.

All blockers found during testing should be compiled in the project's repository issue tracker and labeled with [Messaging-QA] prior to the daily review.

3. Team Member Task Cards

Print or distribute these cards directly to team members. Each contains checkboxes indicating the core criteria to evaluate:

Task Card: Rayne (Sockets & Presence) Assigned
  • Offline States: Disconnect connection during an active chat. Verify that the orange reconnect banner appears.
  • Auto-reconnect Sync: Turn connection back on and confirm that incoming messages queue in chronological order.
  • Typing States: Verify "typing..." appears under the header name when the peer starts typing and clears instantly when they stop or send.
  • Message Actions: Test real-time message editing updates, pinning messages, and deleting messages (must show inline "Message deleted" placeholder).
Task Card: Islam (Media, Audio & Caching) Assigned
  • Hardware Access: Verify camera, microphone, and gallery permission dialogs trigger on new install.
  • Voice Notes: Record a voice clip, test the cancel (trash bin) button, and play back the audio (checking the timer progress indicator).
  • Media Uploads: Send image and video attachments. Ensure they upload successfully to Cloudinary and display without errors.
  • Cache Sync: Reopen the app offline. Verify that the local cache immediately renders channel history and messages.
Task Card: Yassine (Header, Channels & Layout) Assigned
  • DM Identity: Confirm DMs are identified correctly (supporting the uppercase 'DM' database type) and display real partner profile details.
  • Profile sheet redirect: Confirm that clicking on the DM header or partner avatar launches their profile bottom sheet rather than group members list.
  • Symmetry & Width: Ensure long messages wrap correctly within bubbles, occupying at most 75% of screen width to leave professional empty space.
  • Scroll indicator: Ensure the float-to-bottom arrow button behavior works smoothly and clears unread badges.
